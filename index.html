Bio

Background, interests, and current focus.

My background is in computer vision and computational imaging (PhD, University of Kentucky). Recent work emphasizes Space Domain Awareness using unresolved sensing modalities (spectroscopy / photometry) and simulation-driven evaluation. I also work on gamma-ray spectral analysis and sparse reconstruction, including feature-driven methods and hybrid model-based/learned pipelines.

Beyond research, I lead program development in computer science and cybersecurity education and mentor student teams spanning SDA, deep learning, graphics, and immersive systems.
